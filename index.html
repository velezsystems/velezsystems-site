About VAS

Velez Advanced Systems (VAS) is a research-driven robotics company focused on human-scale autonomous systems. We prioritize scalable architectures, efficient power systems, and biologically inspired control strategies.

Our phased prototyping approach ensures each subsystem is validated before full-scale deployment.

Current Project

We are currently in Phase 1 of a humanoid robotics program. This 1:3 scale prototype focuses on gait, balance, and servo control. It integrates:

  • Dynamixel smart servos
  • Solid-state Li-ion battery systems
  • 9-DoF IMUs for orientation and balance
  • Custom spine and joint assembly for realistic motion
